Financial literacy is a problem that is always being overlooked. The financial crisis of 2008 shocked the financial world at all levels had financial literacy as a crucial factor.




Hello there, We are Into the Real World! Here is a little bit about us.


We created an educational app targeted for young individuals across the domains of financial literacy, insurance, housing, budgeting, civic knowledge and self-care. Whether they are leaving their parents home for college, or starting a new business on their own, this app acts as a referential tool for young individuals.
